    Chama Management, Simplified

    Empowering communities, One Chama at a Time

    In today's fast-paced world, managing a Chama can be a complex task. Our application simplifies this process, making it easier for individuals and communities to thrive financially.

    • Our application streamlines the management of Chamas, reducing the time and effort required.
    • By providing a clear overview of financial contributions and growth, we empower members to make informed decisions.
    • Our platform fosters a sense of community, encouraging cooperation and mutual financial growth.

    Our goal is to revolutionize the way Chamas are managed in Kenya, driving financial prosperity and community development. Together, we can build a financially empowered Kenya.

    auto-Disbursement

    Money pooled in merry-go-rounds is automatically disbursed to user' wallets at midnight, ensuring timely payouts without delays.

    Fine Enforcement & Contribution Backdating

    Missed contributions are backdated to the intended recipient, and fines are enforced, ensuring accountability and fair play in all transactions.

    Real-Time Contribution Tracking

    Track every contribution, from member payments to disbursement by managers, with complete transparency for all parties involved.

    ZetuCoins Rewards

    Earn ZetuCoins when you transact from ChamaZetu to Mpesa, offering you extra value for your everyday transactions.

    Rotation Order Visibility

    Stay in the loop by viewing your position in the merry-go-round rotation, knowing when it's your turn to receive.

    Multi-Activity Flexibility

    Create and manage multiple financial activities like table banking, merry-go-rounds, and savings all under one platform.

    Table Banking

    Manage your table banking activities with ease, ensuring that all members are able to contribute and receive funds in a timely manner. Managers can approve, set limits and track all soft loans requested in the table banking activity. Members can request for soft loans and view their loan history.

    chatRoom

    Experience real-time collaboration and community building with our integrated chatroom, designed specifically for Chama members to communicate, share ideas, and grow together.

    52 Weeks Challenge

    Save money weekly for 52 weeks and earn interest on your savings. The 52-week challenge is a fun and easy way to save money and reach your financial goals. Its even better with friends.

    Birthday Chama Activity

    Celebrate your birthday with your chama members by creating a birthday chama activity. Members can contribute towards your birthday gift and you can use the funds to buy a gift of your choice.
